Musicians of Peterborough – Get Involved

Saturday 15th November - come and see us!

OpenCity is an amazing community project for businesses and the people of Peterborough to come together. On the 1st do. A wide range of ideas came up and one of the most popular was the 'DJ Booth' and this is where musicians in Peterborough come in. October we had an inception event where people chose what the screen could.

Here is the plan:
Throughout the week there will be votes on Twitter and through the OpenCity screen to choose the best musician from a wide selection of artists in Peterborough. On Friday at 5pm the music of the chosen artist will be played from the OpenCity screen. Throughout the week there will be vote updates showing who is in the lead.

We are now looking for musicians to be involved in this community project, we have the team to record and upload the music for the musicians – we just need the musicians to get involved.

The screen goes live in February and we have a full 24hrs around Saturday 15th November when we would love for musicians to come along and see us and talk about how they can get involved at this time. We are hoping to have a set up in a separate room so recordings can be done at the time for the screen but those that can’t record on that day will be contacted shortly after to create their music recording by February.

Are you a musician that would like to get involved? Have you seen someone at an Open Mic Night in Peterborough that would be perfect for the event? Maybe you have spotted an amazing busker who you think should get involved?

This project will help brand awareness and growth of musicians fan base for those musicians involved, allowing more people to hear their excellent work.

Please spread the word, we want as many people as possible involved in this amazing community project, so tell every musician you know in Peterborough so we can get started.
