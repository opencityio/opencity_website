Businesses of Peterborough – Get Involved

OpenCity has been formed by a group of people in Peterborough, who want to make Peterborough better. The OpenCity project is putting up an interactive digital screen on Long Causeway in Peterborough. This screen will be used to bring the community of Peterborough together and will provide another channel through which businesses of Peterborough can get to their target market.

The OpenCity team is a group of volunteers, who have been planning, plotting and designing for months, and held an Inception Meeting on 1st October, when businesses and Peterborough people came together to say what they wanted from the screen. On Friday 14th November, Peterborough's techie types are coming together to build the screen. This is where you, as a business in Peterborough come in.

We would like to very kindly ask for donations of food and drink for our Hack-a-thon. We will be in Beckets Chapel, Peterborough Cathedral for 24hrs, from 7pm on Friday 14th November until 7pm Saturday 15th November, and we may need to be fed and watered to keep our energy levels high.

Pav Patel of Peterborough Reuse has very kindly offered bean bags for a Break Zone, for brain refreshing rest-stops throughout the event, and the fab guys of City Fibre have offered drinks and treats for when it's all over, to celebrate our hard work.

We are looking for more friendly businesses in Peterborough who would be able to donate food, drink or treats to the OpenCity team of volunteers, so we can do the best possible job on the OpenCity screen, designed to make Peterborough even better.

If your business or a business you know can donate or sponsor the event we would be very grateful. We will offer Tweetings of thanks in response along with acknowledgements on this very website. Please contact us directly if you can help.
