What is OpenCity?

OpenCity is bringing together digital, creative and ideas people in Peterborough to make our city a better place.
It's a grassroots, volunteer led project, part of Peterborough DNA - the programme shaping a smarter, more sustainable city.
OpenCity is a first for Peterborough, with the area's best creative minds working together and using some pretty cool tech to solve some of the challenges cities like ours face.

Our Aim

Our aim is to identify some of the challenges that face our city and bring together the right people in order to create innovative, digital solutions.
