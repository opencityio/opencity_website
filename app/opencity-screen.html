OpenCity Screen

October 1st 2014 - Peterborough Town Hall

Peterborough DNA, in partnership with a fantastic group of volunteers, launched OpenCity in August 2014 as one of its demonstrator projects. It's unique, it's community led, and it's a first for Peterborough.

OpenCity's first project is to design content for the interactive screen being installed on Long Causeway, Peterborough. We kicked it off with Brainstorming in Peterborough Town Hall 1st October 2014 where the attendees split into groups and faced one of the 4 challenges and responded with a barrage of inspiration and innovation.

The resulting concepts, prioritised by the teams, were pitched back to the whole audience who then took time to vote for their favourites. Those ideas generated a backlog of features that lead to a 24 Hour Hackathon in Peterborough Beckets Chapel 14-15 November 2014. The event was attended by a huge range of people of all ages who all pitched in to help, bringing whatever they could and getting involved. Part of the the programming, interface and visualisation were produced for the interactive screen. We will need to apply the finishing touches to the technology, install and test the system and also finish sourcing the content before the launch in April.
